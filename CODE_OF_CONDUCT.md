# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Rewind Cup is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free
community for everyone, regardless of experience, identity, background, or
ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and specific in technical discussion.
- Critique ideas and changes rather than people.
- Assume good intent while remaining open to correction.
- Cite reliable sources when discussing historical data.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing another person's personal information.
- Help make discussions and contributions accessible to newcomers.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or sustained personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or knowingly false historical claims.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Abusing reporting, review, or moderation systems.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions,
and other spaces where someone represents the Rewind Cup community.

## Reporting and enforcement

Do not litigate sensitive conduct reports in a public issue. Use GitHub's
private vulnerability-reporting channel when available, or privately contact
the maintainer through https://x.com/iamdavidoti.

The maintainer may edit or remove contributions, lock conversations, issue a
warning, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Reports will
be reviewed as privately and promptly as practical. Retaliation against a
reporter or participant in an investigation is prohibited.

Project-maintainer decisions may be appealed once by providing relevant new
context privately. The safety of the community remains the priority.
